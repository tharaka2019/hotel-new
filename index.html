Welcome to Lumière

Nestled in the heart of the city, Lumière represents the pinnacle of fine dining excellence. For nearly a decade, we've been crafting unforgettable culinary experiences that celebrate the finest ingredients sourced from around the globe.

Our chef-led kitchen combines classical French techniques with contemporary innovation, creating dishes that engage all the senses. Every plate is a work of art, meticulously prepared by our award-winning culinary team. We believe in using only the freshest, most sustainable ingredients to ensure every dish reflects our commitment to excellence.

With an extensive wine collection curated by our sommelier, elegant ambiance, and impeccable service, Lumière offers more than just a meal—it's an experience that lingers long after the last course.
