Our Story

Lumière was founded in 2015 with a singular vision: to create a sanctuary of culinary excellence where every detail—from the ambiance to the cuisine—reflects our unwavering commitment to perfection.

Our journey began with Chef Laurent Beaumont's dream of bringing classical French techniques combined with contemporary innovation to the forefront of fine dining. What started as a modest 20-table establishment has grown into one of the city's most celebrated restaurants.

Our Philosophy:

We believe that exceptional dining transcends food alone. It's about creating memories, celebrating moments, and honoring the artistry that goes into every dish and every service.

Today, with a dedicated team of over 50 professionals, we serve hundreds of guests each month, each leaving with an appreciation for the meticulous craftsmanship that defines Lumière.

Our Core Values

Every dish is a masterpiece. We invest in continuous training, innovation, and refinement to maintain the highest standards of culinary excellence.
